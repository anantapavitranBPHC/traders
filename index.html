About Us

Traders@BPHC is a vibrant club dedicated to educating students about trading and finance. We offer workshops, discussions, and networking opportunities to help members enhance their trading skills and financial knowledge.

Mission & Objectives

Our mission is to foster a community of enthusiastic traders and financial enthusiasts. We strive to:

  1. 1) Organize informative workshops and seminars on trading and finance.
  2. 2) Provide a collaborative platform for members to share and refine trading strategies.
  3. 3) Build a supportive network of traders who are passionate about financial markets.

Core team:

We as a core team gather and analyse market data, news, and trends to support the trading strategy. Be a part of learning trading sessions, workshops, and educational resources to enhance trading skills and knowledge.

Design & Video-Editing Team:

As a designer, you'll create visually striking posters for all our on campus events and social media posts and contribute in making merch designs that represent and promote our club. As a Video Editor, your job will be to create video content for/of our events for our social media handles.

Focused on developing a robust, web-based platform, this team will spearhead the design, development, and deployment of our project, ensuring a seamless user experience. Beyond the fest, the Tech Team will continue its mission by automating trading strategies, enhancing efficiency, and working on different algorithms.
